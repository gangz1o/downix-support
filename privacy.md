# Privacy Policy for SeedPilot
**Last Updated: February 25, 2026**

Thank you for choosing SeedPilot. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. SeedPilot is designed as a pure local management tool. Our privacy principle is simple: **We do not collect, store, or share your personal data.**

### 1. Data Collection and Storage
SeedPilot operates completely locally on your device. Any configurations, server addresses, API keys, or download tasks you input are stored strictly on your local device or synced exclusively via your personal Apple iCloud account. The developer has absolutely no access to this data.

### 2. Analytics and Tracking
We respect your digital footprint. SeedPilot does not include any third-party tracking SDKs, advertising trackers, or telemetry systems. We do not monitor your usage behavior.

### 3. In-App Purchases
For optional Pro features, SeedPilot uses Apple's official StoreKit for processing transactions. All payment processing is handled securely by Apple. We do not have access to your credit card information or personal billing details.

### 4. Contact Us
If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: seedpilot@163.com

# SeedPilot 隐私政策
**最近更新日期：2026年2月25日**

感谢您使用 SeedPilot。我们非常重视并致力于保护您的个人隐私。SeedPilot 是一款纯本地运行的管理工具，我们的隐私原则非常简单：**我们不收集、不存储、也不分享您的任何个人数据。**

### 1. 数据的收集与存储
SeedPilot 完全在您的设备本地运行。您在应用内输入的所有配置信息、服务器地址、API 密钥以及下载任务状态，均仅严格保存在您的设备本地，或通过您个人的 Apple iCloud 账户进行同步。开发者无法获取、查看或触碰这些数据。

### 2. 数据分析与追踪
我们尊重您的数字足迹。SeedPilot 内部不包含任何第三方数据分析 SDK、广告追踪器或遥测系统。我们不会追踪您的应用使用行为。

### 3. 应用内购买
如果您选择解锁 Pro 高级功能，SeedPilot 会调用 Apple 官方的 StoreKit 来处理交易。所有的支付环节均由 Apple 安全处理，我们无法获取您的信用卡信息或任何个人账单详情。

### 4. 联系我们
如果您对本隐私政策有任何疑问或建议，请通过以下邮箱联系我们：seedpilot@163.com
